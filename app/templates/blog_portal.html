  • Frustration as Inspiration:

    if a bump in the road makes your day,
    you just might be a developer

    June 23, 2017 | k. anderson

    Glaring sun and choking dust from the dirt parking lot made me squint my eyes as I exited the small suburban office. I quickly scanned the lot for the plate number I’d memorized on my long drive over.

    As my eyes snagged on the correct plate and settled on the faded red sedan, I realized there was a woman behind the wheel. My heart raced and I ran towards the space directly in front of the vehicle, noting triumphantly that the driver was a petite white woman in her 40’s with shoulder-length curly brown hair.

    Two strides in, I realized she had no intention of waiting for me. The car leapt towards me Dukes of Hazzard style...
